Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delicious vegeterian lasgan is soo delicious you will love it too

Ingredients

The Ingredients are as follows :

  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars pasta sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (15 ounce) container part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1.

    Cook the lasagna nooodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or until al dente. Rince, with cold water, and drain.

  2. Step 2.

    In a large saucepan, cook and stir mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and garlic in oil. Stir in pasta sauce and basil; bring to a boil . Reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes.

  3. Step 3.

    Mix together ricotta, 2 cups mozarellaa cheese, and eggs.

  4. Step 4.

    Preheat oven to 350F(175 degrees C). Spread 1 cup tomato suace into the bottom of a greased 9X3 icn baking dish. Layer 1/2 each, lasagna noodles, ricotta mix, sauce, and Parmesan cheese. Repeat layering, and top remaining with 2 cups mozarella cheese.

  5. Step 5.

    Bake uncovered for 40 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
